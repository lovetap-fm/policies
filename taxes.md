# Taxes

*Last updated March 1, 2023*

Taxes are an important part of our present society. Where our products are taxable, we collect and remit consumption tax to governments at the local, state, and/or federal levels. If your billing address is in one of the following jurisdictions, you’ll see an *additional* line item on your invoice for sales tax or VAT. The subscription prices on our product websites are all exclusive of sales tax and VAT.

The tax rate and rules are determined by each jurisdiction and sometimes vary by whether you are using our products for business/commercial or personal purposes. As regulations change, we will update this list.

## Updating your billing address
If you need to change your billing information because you don’t actually reside in a taxable jurisdiction, you can easily handle that from the Stripe Portal link on your Lovetap account page. You may need to re-enter your credit card information as part of the billing address update.

## We’re here to help
Please [contact Lovetap support](help@lovetap.fm) if you have trouble or if:

* you believe you’re being charged in error.
* your organization is tax exempt or has a VAT identification number. Alongside your tax exempt certification or VAT identification number, please include your account’s sign-in email address when you email us.
